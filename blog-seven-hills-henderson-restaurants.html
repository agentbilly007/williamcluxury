Best Restaurants Near Seven Hills,
Henderson NV

By William Crawford May 2, 2026 5 min read

One of the things my clients love most about Seven Hills isn't just the stunning golf course views and custom homes — it's the incredible dining scene right at your doorstep. From upscale chophouses to authentic Neapolitan pizza, Henderson's Seven Hills area has become a genuine food destination. Here's my personal guide to the best spots to eat near one of the Las Vegas Valley's most desirable communities.

Todd's Unique Dining

Fine Dining · Steak & Seafood
4350 E Sunset Rd #102, Henderson, NV 89014 · (702) 259-8633

The gold standard for fine dining in Henderson. Todd's has been a local institution for decades, and for good reason. The menu spans elevated steaks, fresh seafood, and Asian-American fusion dishes like grilled skirt steak and king salmon. If you're taking out-of-town buyers or celebrating a closing, this is the table to book. Upscale but approachable — the kind of place that makes Henderson feel like a real city.

Settebello Pizzeria Napoletana

Italian · Wood-Fired Pizza
The District at Green Valley Ranch, Henderson, NV

Authentic Neapolitan pizza in the heart of Henderson. Settebello imports its traditions straight from Naples — thin, blistered crusts fired in a wood-burning oven, San Marzano tomatoes, fresh mozzarella. The Pizza Pere E Jalepeno is a local favorite, and save room for the Nutella pizza for dessert. Perfect for a casual dinner after a home tour or a weekend night out with the family.

Chinitas Tapas & Sushi

Tapas · Sushi · Asian Fusion
Henderson, NV · Seven Hills Area

A Henderson hidden gem. Chinitas blends Spanish tapas with Japanese sushi in a way that actually works beautifully. The vibe is lively and social — perfect for a night out with neighbors or showing new residents where the locals actually eat. Small plates mean you get to try a lot, and the sushi rolls are consistently excellent. One of those spots that becomes a regular favorite once you discover it.

Juan's Flaming Fajitas & Cantina

Mexican · Tex-Mex
Henderson, NV

If you haven't experienced the tableside fajita experience at Juan's, you're missing out. This Henderson staple is loud, festive, and delicious — the fajitas arrive sizzling and dramatic, and the margaritas are some of the best in the valley. Great for groups, great for families, and great for anyone who just wants generous portions and a fun atmosphere without breaking the bank.

Kyuramen

Japanese · Ramen
Henderson, NV 89052

Ramen done right in Henderson. Kyuramen has built a loyal following with rich, deeply flavored broths, tender chashu pork, and perfectly cooked noodles. The 89052 zip code has become something of a food destination, and Kyuramen is one of the reasons why. Ideal for a cozy weeknight dinner, especially during those rare cool Henderson evenings.

Eureka! Henderson

American · Craft Burgers & Beer
Henderson, NV

For a more casual night out, Eureka! delivers inventive burgers, local craft beers, and a relaxed atmosphere that's been a neighborhood go-to. The menu goes far beyond a standard burger joint — think creative toppings, house-made sauces, and a solid cocktail list. Family-friendly and consistently excellent. A great spot to grab a bite before or after checking out a Seven Hills listing.

Hayworth

American · Upscale Casual
Henderson, NV 89052

A newer addition to the Henderson dining scene that quickly became a standout. Hayworth offers refined American cuisine in a stylish, comfortable setting — think seasonal ingredients, thoughtful presentations, and a wine list that actually impresses. One of the best examples of how Henderson's restaurant scene has matured. If you want something special without the Strip prices or crowds, Hayworth delivers.

Azzurra Cucina Italiana

Italian · Upscale
Henderson, NV

Upscale Italian in a warm, intimate setting. Azzurra is the kind of restaurant you take clients who appreciate the finer things — handmade pastas, classic Italian-American dishes, and a wine list to match. The atmosphere is romantic and the service is attentive. A wonderful dinner option after a sunset showing in Seven Hills.

Echo & Rig

Steakhouse · Butcher
Henderson, NV (Sunset Station area)

A butcher and steakhouse concept that's become one of the top dining destinations in Henderson. Echo & Rig lets you select your cut from the in-house butcher case, then has it prepared to perfection. The charcuterie, cheese boards, and sides are equally impressive. If you're in the mood for a serious steak dinner, this is the place — and it's a genuine point of pride for the local dining scene.

Seven Hills isn't just one of Henderson's most sought-after communities for its gorgeous homes and resort-style living — it's also ideally positioned near some of the best dining the valley has to offer. Whether you're a current resident, considering a move, or visiting the area, these restaurants are worth your time.
