Living in Central Las Vegas

Central Las Vegas is the most diverse real estate market in the valley. Whether you're drawn to the urban energy near the Strip, the family-friendly suburbs of the northwest, or the established neighborhoods of the 89117 zip code, there's a corner of Las Vegas proper for every buyer.

The city offers the broadest range of price points in the metro — from entry-level condos under $200K to custom luxury estates topping $3M. It's also the top choice for investors, with the Strip corridor driving some of the strongest short-term and long-term rental yields in the Southwest.

Why Buy in Central Las Vegas?

  • Most affordable entry points in the Las Vegas Valley
  • Centennial Hills — master-planned, family-friendly, top schools
  • Skye Canyon — newer construction with mountain views and trail access
  • Aliante — established community with golf course and nature discovery park
  • Proximity to Harry Reid International Airport (10–20 min)
  • Strong rental market — especially near UNLV and the Strip corridor
  • Easy I-15, US-95, and I-215 freeway access

Is Las Vegas Right for You?

Central Las Vegas makes the most sense for buyers who want the best price-per-square-foot in the valley, investors looking for rental income, or those who want quick access to the Strip, airports, and major employment hubs. If you're weighing Las Vegas proper against Henderson or Summerlin, the main trade-off is commute convenience vs. neighborhood polish — Las Vegas wins on value and location.
