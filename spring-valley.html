Living in Spring Valley

Spring Valley is an unincorporated community in Clark County, situated just west of the Las Vegas Strip. It's one of the most populated areas in the valley and offers excellent value — you get more home for your dollar compared to Summerlin or Henderson, with the convenience of being minutes from the Strip, major employers, and top-rated schools.

Spring Valley is a favorite among first-time buyers, young professionals, and investors looking for strong rental income in a high-demand area.
