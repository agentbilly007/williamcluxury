Living in Henderson, NV

Henderson is consistently ranked one of the safest and most livable cities in the United States. Located just 16 miles southeast of the Las Vegas Strip, Henderson offers the perfect balance of suburban comfort, city access, and stunning desert scenery.

From the master-planned communities of Green Valley and Anthem to the ultra-luxury enclave of MacDonald Highlands — Henderson has something for every buyer, from first-timers to seasoned investors.

Henderson Real Estate Market

Henderson's real estate market remains strong, driven by consistent population growth, limited inventory, and high demand from California transplants seeking better value. The city offers more space per dollar than comparable markets in California, Arizona, and even parts of Las Vegas proper.

  • Consistently ranked among the safest cities in the US
