Living in Green Valley

Green Valley was one of the first master-planned communities in the Las Vegas Valley, and it shows — mature landscaping, established schools, and a sense of community you don't always find in newer developments. Located in the heart of Henderson, Green Valley offers convenient access to the 215 freeway, McCarran (Harry Reid) Airport, and the Strip.

Homes range from affordable townhomes and condos to larger single-family estates in gated communities. It's a favorite for families, retirees, and investors alike.
