Living in Summerlin

Summerlin is a 22,500-acre master-planned community nestled against the Spring Mountains on the western edge of Las Vegas. With over 230 parks, 150+ miles of trails, award-winning schools, and world-class shopping at Downtown Summerlin, it consistently ranks as one of the best places to live in Nevada.

Whether you're looking for a move-in-ready single-family home, a luxury estate in a guard-gated village, or a brand-new construction, Summerlin has something for every lifestyle and budget — from the $400s to well over $5 million.

Summerlin Real Estate Market

Summerlin remains one of the most competitive markets in Las Vegas. Demand consistently outpaces supply, especially for well-priced detached homes under $800K. New construction from builders like Toll Brothers, Woodside Homes, and Century Communities continues to expand the community westward.

Why Buy in Summerlin?

  • Top-rated Clark County schools including Palo Verde High School
  • Close to Red Rock Canyon National Conservation Area
  • Downtown Summerlin — 125+ shops, restaurants, and entertainment
  • Las Vegas Ballpark — home of the Las Vegas Aviators
  • City National Arena — practice facility of the Vegas Golden Knights
  • Easy freeway access (215 & I-95) with less Strip traffic
  • Low HOA fees for the level of amenities offered
