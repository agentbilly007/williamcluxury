William Crawford Joins
Huntington & Ellis

By William Crawford April 14, 2026 3 min read

Las Vegas, NV — William Crawford, luxury real estate agent and founder of the William C. Luxury brand, is proud to announce his affiliation with Huntington & Ellis, A Real Estate Agency — one of the highest-producing and most respected brokerages in the state of Nevada.

About Huntington & Ellis

Huntington & Ellis has earned its reputation as one of Las Vegas's top real estate brokerages through a combination of elite agent talent, cutting-edge technology, and an unwavering commitment to client results. With multiple offices across the Las Vegas Valley and a roster of top-producing agents, the brokerage consistently ranks among Nevada's highest-volume firms.

What sets Huntington & Ellis apart is its culture: a collaborative environment where agents are equipped with world-class marketing resources, transaction support, and the infrastructure to serve clients at the highest level. The brokerage's reach extends across all major Las Vegas submarkets — Summerlin, Henderson, Green Valley, the luxury corridor, and new construction communities throughout the valley.

Why Huntington & Ellis

"Joining Huntington & Ellis was a clear decision," says William Crawford. "When you're committed to delivering a luxury experience for every client — whether they're buying their first home or their fifth — you need to be aligned with a brokerage that operates at that same standard. Huntington & Ellis gives me the platform to do that at scale."

The affiliation brings together Crawford's local market expertise, his established client base across the Las Vegas Valley, and the brokerage's industry-leading tools and transaction systems — creating a best-in-class experience for buyers and sellers at every price point.

What This Means for Clients

For current and future clients of William Crawford, the Huntington & Ellis affiliation means:

  • Greater marketing reach — properties listed with William receive exposure through Huntington & Ellis's brokerage-wide network and marketing channels
  • Transaction support — a full support team behind every deal, ensuring smooth closings from contract to keys
  • Market intelligence — access to brokerage-wide data and insights across all Las Vegas submarkets
  • Same personal service — William remains your direct point of contact throughout every transaction, with the added resources of a top-tier brokerage behind him

Serving Las Vegas & Henderson

William Crawford continues to serve buyers, sellers, and investors across the greater Las Vegas Valley — including Summerlin, Henderson, Green Valley, Spring Valley, and Central Las Vegas. He specializes in luxury residential real estate, new construction, and relocation clients coming from California and other high-cost markets.

For buyers, William offers full buyer representation at no cost — his commission is paid by the seller. For sellers, he provides professional photography, targeted digital marketing, and data-driven pricing strategy designed to maximize net proceeds.
